Your Story Deserves a
Hollywood Ending

Latinos have stories to tell, yet they make up less
than 1% of the ones told in Hollywood. As a champion
of the Hispanic community McDonald’s wants to help
change that. With Spotlight Dorado, McDonald’s is
giving aspiring Latino filmmakers a chance to
participate in a short film competition. Aspiring
filmmakers will bring their vision to life having their
story produced into a short film with invaluable
mentorship from renowned industry leaders and a
chance to showcase their film in film festivals around
the country. Tell us your story, the world needs to
experience it!*

*Must be 18 and resident of 50 US/DC to enter. Enter by June 3, 2022. For Official Rules,
prize details and how to enter visit www.SpotlightDorado.com. Sponsored by
McDonald's USA.
